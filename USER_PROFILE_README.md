## 🔭 About Me

I am a Legal Tech developer and researcher dedicated to building **Open Source Artificial Intelligence tools for Brazilian jurisprudence and legal practice**. My mission is to make Brazilian case law, qualified precedents, and legal reasoning accessible, fast, and transparent through generative AI.

- 🤖 **Focus Area**: Open Source Legal AI, Jurisprudence ETL Pipelines, RAG for Brazilian Courts (STJ / STF / TJs).
- 🛠️ **Tech Stack**: React 18, Vite, Node.js, Python, Google Gemini AI API, TailwindCSS / Custom CSS Systems.
- 💬 **Ask me about**: STJ Qualified Precedents, Legal RAG, Jurisprudence Scraping, Open Source Legal AI in Brazil.

## 🚀 Featured Open Source Legal AI Projects

### ⚖️ [STJ Precedentes Atlas](https://github.com/ricarossetto/stj-precedentes-app)
> **Open Source Qualified Precedents & Legal AI Platform for STJ Jurisprudence**
- Automated harvesting of **1,190 binding STJ precedents** (*Temas Repetitivos, Controvérsias, IAC, SIRDR, PUIL*) in Civil & Social Security Law.
- Real-time natural language query analysis via **Google Gemini 2.5 AI**.
- Pre-computed plain-language legal summaries, sub-area categorizations, and CSV data export.
- 🔗 **Live App**: [stj-precedentes-app.vercel.app](https://stj-precedentes-app.vercel.app)
